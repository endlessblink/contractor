# Purpose

Generate polished Hebrew-first business documents with reliable RTL layout and readable mixed Hebrew/English content.

# Core Rules

- Write in natural professional Hebrew, not translated English.
- Keep the document concise and scannable.
- Use clear section headings and short paragraphs.
- Keep legal/business wording factual and direct.
- Do not add marketing fluff, greetings, or closing sales copy unless explicitly requested.
- Preserve English brand names, product names, URLs, emails, and technical terms exactly.

# RTL And Mixed Text Rules

- Hebrew sentences stay RTL.
- Standalone emails, URLs, GitHub links, LinkedIn links, and phone numbers should remain visually LTR.
- Mixed technical terms inside Hebrew sentences are acceptable when the term is the natural industry wording.
- Avoid awkward Hebrew translations for common tool names such as GitHub, ComfyUI, Claude, MCP, Remotion, Premiere, Resolve, or Midjourney.

# Structure Rules

- Prefer stable, predictable sections over decorative layouts.
- Use bullets for lists of deliverables, experience, tools, responsibilities, and terms.
- Keep each bullet focused on one idea.
- Put important details early in each section.
- Use dates consistently across the document.

# Quality Bar

- The output should be ready for DOCX generation, not just chat text.
- Avoid duplicate sections and repeated claims.
- Avoid vague adjectives unless backed by concrete details.
- Check Hebrew spelling, grammar, punctuation, and phrasing before final output.
